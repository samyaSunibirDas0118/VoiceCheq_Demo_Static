About

Why VoiceCheq

VoiceCheq is building a voice-native layer for peer-to-peer payments — a way to move money with a single spoken command instead of a sequence of taps, screens, and confirmations.

VoiceCheq aims to set the new standard in p2p transactions by giving the users the experience of a fully actualized voice payments system
